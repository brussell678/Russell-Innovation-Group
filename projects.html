PowerBI Materiel Readiness Brief (MRB)

This internal Business Intelligence initiative was developed to enhance efficiency in materiel readiness.

The project transforms maintenance data management from traditional, repetitive manual processing into a more agile, servant-focused approach.

  • Significant Time Savings: Reduces the time Marines dedicate to pulling reports and building workbooks from 35.5 hours to a target end state of 2.5 hours.
  • Improved Accuracy: Automation mitigates the likelihood of human error and addresses shortfalls caused by out-of-date data inaccuracies.
  • Sustainability and Adaptability: Designed for implementation by any unit with minimal Power BI proficiency and no specialized training.
  • Collaborative Customization: Units can adopt the foundational `.PBIX` file and tailor it to fit their specific operational preferences.
  • Strategic Foundation for AI: Provides an accessible entry point into complex technology and supports future AI adoption across the Marine Corps.

CGIP AI Assistant

AI-enabled preparation tool for Marine Corps CGIP inspections, generating dynamic checklists with citation-backed references from official directives.

Delivered a customizable AI workflow that transforms inspection guidelines into interactive checklists, ensuring traceability to sources like MCO 5040.3 and NAVMC 3500 series for consistent, compliant readiness.

  • Reduced preparation time by automating checklist generation across admin, logistics, and training areas.
  • Improved inspection consistency with direct citations and hyperlinks (under DoD constraints).
  • Enabled rapid updates through modular reference ingestion.
  • Lightweight LLM integration for accurate, source-grounded outputs.
  • Preprocessing for structured data from unit SOPs and manuals.
  • Optimized for DoD environments with open-source tech stack for minimal costs.

MilVector AI (In Development)

AI-enabled career transition platform for U.S. military service members translating military experience into civilian career intelligence and actionable employment pathways.

Developing a structured AI workflow that analyzes military service records and professional history to generate targeted resumes, career pathways, and transition timelines aligned with each user's goals and separation date.

  • Translates military occupational experience into civilian-aligned skills and competencies using AI-assisted interpretation of VMET, JST, and service documentation.
  • Generates tailored resumes and job application materials optimized for modern hiring systems and applicant tracking software.
  • Provides a milestone-based transition roadmap aligned to a service member's projected end-of-service date.
  • Recommends certifications, education pathways, and networking actions to strengthen career positioning.
  • Secure document ingestion with guidance for redacting sensitive information prior to upload.
  • Designed as a scalable web platform capable of supporting thousands of transitioning service members annually.
